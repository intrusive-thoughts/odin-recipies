Onigiri (Japanese Rice Ball)

Description

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice or Short-Grain Rice: 2 cups, cooked and slightly cooled
  • Fillings: Options include cooked salmon, tuna mixed with mayonnaise, pickled plum, or any preferred filling
  • Nori: Sheets cut into strips or bands, for wrapping
  • Salt: To taste
  • Water: For wetting hands to prevent sticking

Steps

  1. Prepare Rice

    2. Cook the rice according to package instructions. Let it cool slightly until it's comfortable to handle but still warm.

  2. Prepare Fillings

    3. While the rice is cooling, prepare your chosen fillings, cutting them into small pieces if necessary.

  3. Shape Onigiri

    4. Wet your hands with water and sprinkle them with a bit of salt. Take a handful of rice and form a hollow center for the filling. Place your filling in the center, then add a bit more rice on top. Gently press and shape the rice into a triangle, ball, or cylinder.

  4. Wrap with Nori

    5. Wrap the shaped onigiri with a strip of nori for easier handling and flavor. The nori can be added just before eating to keep it crisp.

  5. Serve

    6. Onigiri can be enjoyed immediately or packed as a snack or meal for later. It's a versatile and satisfying option that showcases the simple beauty of Japanese cuisine.
